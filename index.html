Technology Services

Timoltech's core business revolves around its technology solutions, which are tailored to help clients identify issues, and to provide end-to-end solutions for them. These include design, development, and implementation of solutions, as well as IT systems integration and Application development.

Outsourcing

Timoltech's outsourcing services allow clients individually or in groups to hand over full or partial control of IT systems for applications and infrastructures, along with associated business processes.

Business Consulting

With digital and tech strategy getting ever closer to the core of many businesses' key priorities, Timoltech also offers services tailored towards the C-suite through its Consulting unit. The unit helps businesses to perform digital transformation programs that improve growth and provide a competitive advantage. Key issues handled by the group include data and analytics, digital transformation, and strategy.
